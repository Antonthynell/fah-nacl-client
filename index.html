You are contributing to project 2968.

This project folds a small beta-hairpin peptide, trpzip2. This peptide is a model system that has been studied by many other groups. Here, we aim to simulate the IR spectra of trpzip2 as it folds. We are studying this with two different projects: 2968 and 2969, each at different temperatures of 300 and 350 Kelvin, respectively.

This project is managed by Prof. Xuhui Huang at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.
